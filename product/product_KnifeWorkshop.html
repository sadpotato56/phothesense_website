Forge in Fire – Knife Making Workshop in Da Sy Village

Step into a centuries-old blacksmith village on Hanoi’s outskirts and forge your own knife with Vietnam’s only female master artisan of Da Sy.

About this activity

  • Duration: 2.5–3 hours
  • Location: Da Sy blacksmith village, Hanoi (craft since late 16th–early 17th century)
  • Master Artisan: Vietnam knife-making artisan in the village
  • Private or small group workshop
  • Guide & host: English, Vietnamese
  • Traditional home-workshop where the forge burns day and night
  • Free cancellation up to 24 hours in advance

In this hands-on knife making workshop, you’ll join their daily rhythm of fire and steel. Guided step by step by the artisan and your local tour guide, you’ll transform a simple piece of steel into a personal knife: heating it in the roaring forge, hammering it to shape, sharpening it on the grinding stone, carving and fitting your own handle and finally engraving your name on the blade. By the end, you won’t just bring home a knife — you’ll carry a story of resilience, craft, and the joy of creation.

The Journey

Welcome to Da Sy Village

Arrive at the historic blacksmith village. Meet your host at the Artisan House, enjoy a welcome tea, and hear stories about the village's 400-year history.

Fire & Steel (The Forge)

Step into the workshop. Learn safety techniques, select your steel, and begin the heating and hammering process under the master's guidance.

Shaping & Sharpening

Sharpen your blade using traditional grinding stones. Watch the master applies a heat treating technique passed down through generations to enhance the steel's durability and ensure a longer-lasting edge.

Crafting the Handle & Finishing

Carve and fit a wooden handle to your knife. Perform final polishing and engraving your name (optional).
