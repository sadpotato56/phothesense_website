The Journey

The Hustle: Market & The Ghost Cart

Start at Dong Xuan Market to understand the source of ingredients. Then, weave through the traffic to catch the legendary "Ghost Cart" before they sell out. Taste the urgency and freshness of street life.

The Legacy: A French Touch

Slow down at a family-run bakery that has stood still in time. Taste a traditional Banh Mi that honors the original French baguette—crispy crust, airy crumb, and homemade cold cuts.

The Twist: Savory & Sweet

Time to experiment. We’ll try a regional variation from Central Vietnam (spicier, bolder) and then refresh your palate with a unique Sweet Banh Mi that locals love.

The Reflection: Hidden Rooftop

Climb the stairs of an old building to a secret cafe. Sip on a signature Egg Coffee or Tea, overlook the Hoan Kiem Lake, and reflect on the flavors of the day.
